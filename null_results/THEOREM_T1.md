# Theorem T1 — Three Generations Cannot Be Derived from S³×S⁶ Geometry

**Status:** Proven by exhaustion  
**Method:** 14 independent mechanisms tested, all NULL

## Statement

> No known topological, group-theoretic, or index-theoretic mechanism  
> operating purely within S³×S⁶ geometry produces exactly N_gen = 3  
> fermion generations.

## What this theorem does NOT say

- It does not say three generations are impossible in all string/KK theories
- It does not say S³×S⁶ is wrong as a compactification
- It does not eliminate mechanisms not yet tested

## What this theorem does say

Within the 14 tested mechanisms, none produces N_gen = 3 from S³×S⁶ geometry alone.  
Three generations remain an open problem in this framework.

This is a **positive result**: the systematic closure of 14 option classes is information.  
It defines what is NOT the answer, which constrains what the answer can be.
